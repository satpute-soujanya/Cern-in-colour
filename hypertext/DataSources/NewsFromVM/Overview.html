CERN News groups

Do not confuse CERN news with Internet news .The CERN internal news system contains groups which are public, and those which are private. You can find the public ones from the list. There are other private groups not mentioned there: to access them you need to know the name. If you want to access a private group, you can do it explicitly with the command www "http://crnvmc.cern.ch./NEWS/<groupname>" where is the name of your private group, or a number of groups separated by plus signs. ("Quotes" necessary on VMS only.) You can build links to private groups from your group home page. __________________________________________________________
